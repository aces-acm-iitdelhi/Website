We are

ACES-ACM, the official ACM student chapter of IIT Delhi and winner of the Best Departmental Society Award (AY 2023-24), caters to all facets of a CS enthusiast's life. From all-night coding marathons to gaming tournaments, we organize a diverse range of events. Our technical highlights include Digital Enigma, Code Hunt, Lingo-De-Tryst, hackathons, and sponsored competitions like the Jane Street Estimathon, Tower Research Challenge, and Delhi FOSS 2.0. On the fun side, we host the Departmental Trek, Freshers' Party, gaming tournaments, and more. Informative events such as FROSH, Intern ke Fundae, and Research ke Fundae ensure holistic growth for the student community.

ABOUT IIT DELHI

Indian Institute of Technology Delhi is one of the 23 IITs created to be Centres of Excellence for training, research and development in science, engineering and technology in India.

ABOUT ACM

ACM, the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, delivers resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. ACM provides the computing field's premier Digital Library and serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources.

OUR PURPOSE

We host a blend of events, from coding challenges, hackathons, and industry-sponsored competitions to gaming tournaments and social gatherings. Our initiatives ensure learning and growth. With a balance of fun and skill-building, we inspire innovation and camaraderie in the student community.
